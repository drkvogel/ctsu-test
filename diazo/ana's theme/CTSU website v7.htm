About CTSU

CTSU’s work chiefly involves studies of the causes and treatment of “chronic” diseases such as cancer,                    heart attack or stroke (which, collectively, account for most adult deaths worldwide), although it does also involve some studies of other major conditions in developed and developing countries.

Large scale randomised and observational evidence is needed to investigate appropriately reliably the causes, prevention and treatment of premature death and disability.  The CTSU experience shows that such studies can be practicable and relatively inexpensive if sufficient attention is paid to the details of study design.  Read more
